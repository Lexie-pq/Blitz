## 🌟 Project Overview
**Blitz** is a high-fidelity smart companion application prototype designed for the premium electric bicycle brand **BESV**. Based on BESV's philosophy of "Beauty, Eco-friendly, Smarter, and Vision," Blitz bridges the gap between cycling hardware and a digital lifestyle.

This project is a fully interactive **Figma** prototype connected to a live-responsive database. While the data is currently **Mock-up (demo data)**, the app demonstrates full operational logic—from real-time riding stats to a complex social ecosystem and green energy gamification.

### 🎥 Promotional Power
To showcase the integration of cutting-edge technology, the project's **promotional video was co-created using OpenAI's Sora**. This demonstrates our ability to leverage Generative AI to visualize complex brand visions and software experiences efficiently.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Main Navigation & Dashboard
The central hub for users.
* **Device Info:** Real-time display of BESV bike battery level and mileage.
* **Navigation:** Integrated map for active cycling routes.
* **Navigation Bar:** Quick access to Statistics, Activities, Community, and personal profile (Me).

### 2. Multi-Dimensional Activities
* **City Check-in (Cultural Tourism):** Encourages urban exploration by checking in at famous landmarks to earn exclusive rewards.
* **Carbon Footprint Ranking:** Calculates carbon savings based on distance and speed. Users earn badges and compete in local rankings to promote eco-friendly habits.
* **Green Energy Exchange:** Converts "kinetic energy" (climbing height and distance) into virtual energy points. These points can be used to redeem battery packs or support public welfare energy projects.
* **Supply Stations:** Real-time map view of nearby rest stops, maintenance points, and available supplies.

### 3. Community Hub
A dedicated social platform for BESV riders. Connect with others based on geographic location or shared interests, share posts, and join local riding groups.

### 4. Smart Statistics & Personalization
* **Riding Data:** Visualization of historical riding stats (Demo data).
* **Personal Center:** Manage profile settings, view earned badges, and track performance metrics like CO2 saved and calories burned.

    * **Access:** Login, Sign Up, and Guest Mode.

## 🌟 项目简介
**Blitz** 是一款专为高端电动自行车品牌 **BESV** 打造的高保真智能伴侣 App 原型。基于 BESV “美丽、环保、智能、愿景” 的品牌哲学，Blitz 实现了骑行硬件与数字化生活的深度连接。

本项目通过 **Figma** 构建，并连接了动态响应数据库。虽然目前的骑行数据为 **Mock（模拟）数据**，但已完整实现了从骑行监控、社交互动到绿色能源激励系统的全部操作逻辑。

## 🚀 核心功能介绍

### 1. 主导航仪表盘 (Main Page)
用户最初的导航页面，集成了核心设备信息。
* **状态监控：** 顶部实时显示 BESV 自行车的电量、骑行里程等硬件数据。
* **实时导航：** 页面中部集成骑行地图导航。
* **底部导航：** 快速切换数据统计 (Statistics)、活动 (Activities)、社区 (Community) 及个人中心 (Me)。

### 2. 核心活动模块 (Activities)
* **城市打卡 (文旅结合)：** 鼓励用户探索所在城市的著名景点，通过打卡获得实物或虚拟奖励。
* **碳足迹排名：** 根据骑行距离与速度计算碳减排量，并以此设计勋章系统与排名，激励绿色出行。
* **绿能收集兑换：** 强调“运动产生能量”，将爬升高度与距离转化为能量值，用于兑换电池包或参与公益能源项目。
* **驿站补给：** 智能展示附近的补给站点及物资情况，为长途骑行提供保障。

### 3. 社交社区 (Community)
为全球用户创造交流平台。支持根据地理位置或注册特征添加好友，具备发帖、点赞、评论等完整的社交互动功能。

### 4. 数据统计与个人中心 (Me)
* **统计模块：** 以图表形式展示历史骑行轨迹与性能表现。
* **个人页面：** 管理个人信息，查看获得的勋章、节能总量及卡路里消耗。

---

## 🛠️ 技术实现与架构
* **设计工具：** 使用 **Figma** 进行高保真原型设计，包含复杂的交互组件。
* **信息架构 (IA)：**
    * **入口：** 支持登录、注册及访客模式 (Guest)。
    * **核心流：** 开始骑行 (模拟数据) -> 活动参与 -> 社区分享。
    * **数据库连接：** 实现了数据库与 UI 组件的连接，支持动态数据展示（目前为 Demo 数据）。
